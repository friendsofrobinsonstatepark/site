From I-91: Take exit 3 off I-91. Go over the South End Bridge, staying to the right, and merge onto the rotary. Follow the rotary around to Rt. 57. From 57, take the exit marked “Main St. – Agawam/W. Springfield”. Follow Main Street and continue onto Springfield Street. At the fork near CVS, bear right. The park will be about a half mile further on the right.
